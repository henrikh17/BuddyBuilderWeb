Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BuddyBuilder. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our iOS application.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address, name, and profile pictures provided during registration.
  • Location Data: We request location access to show you nearby workout groups, events, and matches. This is only used while the app is in use.
  • Fitness Data: If you connect third-party services like Strava, we sync your workout data to provide our core social fitness features.
  • Device Data: Basic analytics and crash reports to improve app stability.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to provide, operate, and maintain the BuddyBuilder experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time directly within the BuddyBuilder app via the settings menu. All data is wiped from our servers immediately upon deletion.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: registration@buddybuilder.ch
